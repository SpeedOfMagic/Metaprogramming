struct G\+Lib\+::\+D\+F\+S$<$ cur\+\_\+node, graph, visited\+\_\+nodes $>$}

Performs Depth-\/\+First Search, starting from passed vertex. It doesn\textquotesingle{}t visit vertexes that have been visited already. It returns visited edges in chronological order, from which it\textquotesingle{}s easy to deduce \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_g_lib_1_1_d_f_s}{D\+FS}}. It\textquotesingle{}s more versatile than one may think) Also a variation of Composite pattern.
